# Visuae

Visuae is an educational technology startup founded by students at Queen's University Belfast. We are dedicated to transforming the learning experience through innovative technology solutions.

## About Us

We believe in making education more accessible, engaging, and effective through the power of technology. Our team of passionate students is committed to developing tools and platforms that enhance the educational journey for learners everywhere.
